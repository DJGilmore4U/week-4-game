Simple Crystals Game

This is how you play the game!


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Reach"
  2. You will find the crystals below.
    • BLUE
    • PINK
    • PURPLE
    • WHITE
  3. Each Crystal has a mystery point value.
  4. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total.
  5. You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly generated number.
  6. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random generated number.
  7. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  8. Each time when the game begins, the point value of each crystal will change!
